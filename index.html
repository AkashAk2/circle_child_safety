Child Safety Standards

Circle Connect’s published standards to prevent Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE)

Our Commitment

Circle Connect is a private, invitation‑only social app. Users can join circles only by sending or accepting invitations. While this closed model reduces exposure to strangers and public distribution, we maintain a zero‑tolerance policy toward any form of Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM) or exploitation.

Standards & Enforcement

  • Zero Tolerance: CSAM and child exploitation are strictly prohibited. Accounts involved will be permanently suspended.
  • Content Handling: We act promptly on credible notices relating to CSAM, including restricting access, preserving evidence where required by law, and disabling accounts.
  • Lawful Reporting: We comply with applicable child-protection and reporting laws and will report suspected CSAM to the relevant national or regional authorities as required.
  • Safety by Design: Circle Connect limits sharing to known contacts via invitation and acceptance, reducing unsolicited contact and discovery.

Reporting Concerns

Because Circle Connect is a private, invite‑only app, there is no in‑app “Report” button. To report a child safety concern related to activity on Circle Connect, please email:

contactaudevs@gmail.com

Include any relevant details (usernames, circle name, dates/times, and a description of the concern). Do not send or forward illegal content.

Compliance

Circle Connect complies with applicable laws and will report suspected CSAM to the appropriate authorities consistent with legal obligations. Where permissible and safe, we may preserve minimal information needed to support reports made to authorities.
